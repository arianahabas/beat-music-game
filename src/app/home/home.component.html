Beat the Beat

Unleash Your Inner Music Guru

How to Play:

  • Step 1: "Select Your Beat: Choose from a variety of genres to tailor your musical journey."
  • Step 2: "Guess the Track: Listen to short snippets and guess the song title or artist name."
  • Step 3: "Rack Up Points: The quicker and more accurately you guess, the higher you score."
  • Step 4: "Claim Your Spot: Enter your name on the leaderboard and see how you stack up against other music aficionados."
